A modern movie town in the heart of Kolkata

AN IDEAL STUDIO FOR MAKING MOVIE AND TELEVISION PROJECT, BOTH FICTION AND NON-FICTION.

Chitrayan Film Studio, situated at a very close peaceful place to the city of Kolkata, provides all modern and sophisticated facilities to cater to all the needs of a production house in making the movies, television projects, both fiction and non-fiction, in a better arena. Easily accessible by different modes of transport, Chitrayan Film Studio is proving to be the first choice of all the production houses.

With air-conditioned shooting floor equipped with all required modern amenities and latest fire fighting set-up, adequate number of air-conditioned make-up rooms with attached toilets, air-conditioned production control rooms, rooms for site production office, canteen, dormitory, glass room, common toilets for both gentlemen and ladies and huge parking space this film studio takes care of all the needs of the production houses and provides them a hustle free environment for shooting.
