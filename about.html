who we are

A film studio for all your needs.

Chitrayan Film Studio is the latest addition to the list of film studios in Kolkata. Easily accessible by cars, buses and auto-rickshaws Chitrayan Film Studio, with a dynamic management team and a neat, clean and peaceful environment, provides all the requirements, expected by a production house. Different production houses have already successfully completed their shooting of feature films and television projects, both fiction and non-fiction.
