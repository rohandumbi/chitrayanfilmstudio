Leader in providing shooting locations for producing feature films, made for TV movies, dramatic, comedy and reality television series, as well as, factual and lifestyle programming.

Situated very close to the city, Chitrayan Film Studio provides modern and sophisticated facilities to cater to all your needs. Conveniently located and accessible through several modes of transport, Chitrayan is proving to be the first choice of all major production houses.

A shooting floor equipped with modern technologies, power back up, dressing rooms, plenty of parking space, this studio takes care of all your concerns and provides you a hassle free environment for your shooting. One stop solution for all your shooting needs.
