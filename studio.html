Studio Floor

A huge 120 feet by 85 feet air conditioned shooting floor.

The spacious shooting floor is acoustically treated in a proper way as per modern technology and has adequate arrangements to suspend lights as per requirements of a production house.
Multiple sets can be constructed on this spacious floor and thus a production house can continue shooting of different scenes of a movie, television project, both fiction and non-fiction, uninterruptedly minimizing the cost of production.
Several production houses have already been benefitted using this modern shooting floor.

Catwalk.

Catwalks being the essential part of the shooting floor for movement of the technicians to suspend lights and microphones etc., directed at the particular shooting spot, Chitrayan Film Studio has constructed wide catwalks with railing all through under the direct supervision of the renowned structural engineers to ensure safe and easy movement.
