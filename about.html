who we are

State of the art film studio for all your needs.

Chitrayan Film Studio is one of the latest additions to the list of film studios in Kolkata. It has all the facilities and services expected of a modern day studio. With spacious make-up rooms, a huge shooting floor, neat and clean environment Chitrayan keeps all your requirements in mind. Be it films, reality shows or serials, Chitrayan is bound to impress you. Being strategically located, it is accessible via private car, public buses, auto rickshaws and metro (under construction). There have already been shootings of Bengali films, reality shows and serials. An experienced management team is ever present to cater to your demands.
